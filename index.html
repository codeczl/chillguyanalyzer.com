Game Description

In Ball in Cup, you need to construct the playing field using wooden planks and glass. The goal is to guide the ball along a designed path until it successfully drops into the cup. Master the Ball in Cup mechanics to conquer each challenging level.

How to Play

  • Initial Setup: After completing the initial installation, you can adjust the piece placement
  • Level Design: When the path is ready, click the ball to release it and observe its trajectory
  • Advanced Elements: Higher levels introduce fans and magnets in addition to basic wooden planks and glass
  • Retry System: If you fail, you can restart the current level
  • Level Progress: You can return to any previously completed level at any time

Game Tips

  • Carefully observe the start and end points of each Ball in Cup level
  • Make strategic use of gravity to design optimal ball trajectories
  • Pay attention to angles when placing wooden planks and glass
  • When stuck, experiment with different adjustments to find the best solution

Progressive Challenges

As you advance in Ball in Cup, the difficulty increases with:

  • More complex level layouts
  • Precise trajectory calculations
  • Strategic use of special items like fans and magnets
  • Time-based challenges
